# HomeBase

## Mission

Help children become capable, kind, responsible adults through consistent expectations, positive reinforcement, and age-appropriate financial education.

HomeBase is not only an allowance tracker. It is a family system for teaching responsibility, financial literacy, kindness, persistence, and contribution.

## Core Philosophy

- High warmth and high expectations.
- Children have a voice, but parents remain responsible for final decisions.
- Love, attention, and belonging are unconditional.
- Privileges, trust, and spending choices can be connected to responsibility.
- The app supports parenting. It does not replace judgment.
- Consequences should be predictable, proportional, and tied to meaningful behavior.
- Good choices create opportunities.
- We do not hurt people with our hands or our words.
- We respect people and places.
- We clean up after ourselves wherever we are.
- We leave people, places, and situations better than we found them.
- Nobody is expected to be perfect. Everyone is expected to keep learning and improving.

## Family Values

### Be Kind
We do not use our hands or words to hurt people.

### Tell the Truth
Mistakes can be fixed. Dishonesty makes fixing them harder.

### Respect People and Places
We clean up after ourselves at home, in cars, at other people’s homes, at parks, beaches, restaurants, and public spaces.

### Contribute
Everyone helps because everyone is part of the family.

### Keep Learning
Reading, language learning, music, sports, curiosity, creativity, and practice all count.

### Earn Opportunities
Good choices build trust, freedom, and responsibility.

### Keep Growing
We learn from mistakes and try again.

## Monthly Money System

Each child starts each month with a $40 monthly budget.

Rewards and deductions adjust the final amount.

At the end of the month, children can choose how much goes toward:

- Available money
- Savings toward a major item
- Buy something now
- Cash out

Children can split the money across these options however they choose.

## Event Structure

Events use two parts:

### Part A: Event Behavior Bonus
Earned for respectful behavior, patience, following directions, and not repeatedly complaining.

### Part B: Helper Bonus
Additional money can be earned for carrying supplies, helping younger siblings, setting up, packing up, or going above and beyond.

## Transaction Record

Every entry should automatically save:

- Date and time
- Child
- Parent who entered it
- Reward or deduction
- Category
- Activity
- Quantity
- Price each
- Total amount
- Optional note

Notes are required for Other.

History should not be deleted. Mistakes should be corrected with a correction entry.
