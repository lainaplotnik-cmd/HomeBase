# HomeBase Family Values

These are not just rules.

They are the principles we want to live by every day.

The purpose of HomeBase is not to create perfect children.

The purpose is to help each of us become capable, kind, responsible people who leave the world a little better than we found it.

---

# ❤️ Be Kind

We treat people with kindness and respect.

We do not hurt people with our hands.

We do not hurt people with our words.

Kindness does not mean weakness.

It means choosing to make someone else's day a little better whenever we can.

Examples:

- Speak respectfully.
- Encourage others.
- Help someone who is struggling.
- Think before you speak.

---

# 🤝 Tell the Truth

Everyone makes mistakes.

Mistakes can be fixed.

Lying makes fixing them harder.

Being honest builds trust.

Trust is one of the most valuable things a person can earn.

---

# 🌎 Respect People and Places

We respect our home.

We respect other people's homes.

We respect schools.

We respect parks.

We respect beaches.

We respect restaurants.

We respect our car.

We respect public places.

We clean up after ourselves.

Whenever possible...

**We leave people, places, and situations better than we found them.**

---

# 💪 Contribute

Everyone in our family helps.

Helping is not something we do because we get paid.

Helping is something we do because we are part of a family.

Sometimes HomeBase rewards extra effort.

But contributing to our family is part of who we are.

---

# 📚 Keep Learning

Learning never stops.

Read books.

Ask questions.

Practice.

Learn languages.

Play music.

Try new things.

Be curious.

Progress matters more than perfection.

---

# 💰 Earn Opportunities

Money is not the goal.

Responsibility is.

HomeBase uses money to help teach real-life skills.

Good choices build trust.

Trust leads to freedom.

Freedom comes with responsibility.

---

# 🌱 Keep Growing

Nobody is perfect.

Not kids.

Not parents.

Not anyone.

We will all make mistakes.

What matters is learning from them, making things right when we can, and trying again tomorrow.

---

# ❤️ Family Before Everything

We look out for one another.

We celebrate each other's successes.

We help each other through difficult times.

We solve problems together.

We remember that we are on the same team.

---

# Our Goal

When you grow up, we hope you are known as someone who:

- tells the truth
- keeps their word
- works hard
- continues learning
- treats people with kindness
- respects the world around them
- contributes wherever they go
- leaves people, places, and situations better than they found them

If HomeBase helps you become that kind of person, then it has done its job.
